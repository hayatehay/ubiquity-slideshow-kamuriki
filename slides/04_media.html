Enjoy your music and videos

Listen to your favourite music with the Rhythmbox, a simple, robust music player, and watch a variety of videos with the VLC, which supports many formats.

Additional codecs can be supported by installing the lubuntu-restricted-extras package.

Included software

  • Rhythmbox

  • VLC media player

Supported software

  • Audacious

  • Gnome-Mplayer
